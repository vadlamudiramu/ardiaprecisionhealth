How It's Built

Ardia's Neuro-Symbolic Sandwich Architecture combines three AI layers to produce explainable, citation-backed clinical appeal narratives at scale.

🧠
Layer 1 — Neural (LLM)
Anthropic Claude as the clinical reasoning engine. Reads EHR notes, lab results, and genomic reports. Generates human-quality narrative arguments with medical citations.

Layer 2 — Symbolic (Rules Engine)
847+ payer-specific LCD/NCD rules. Real-time CMS policy database sync. Validates every claim against the exact rules payers use to deny it — so our AI argues the right fight.
