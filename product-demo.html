    Appeal Generator

    Paste any denied claim. The Ardia Neuro-Symbolic AI engine — a three-layer system combining Claude (Anthropic LLM), 847 symbolic policy rules, and ML denial prediction — generates a clinical-grade appeal brief in under 90 seconds.
