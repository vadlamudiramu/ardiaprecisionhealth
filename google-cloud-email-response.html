Our original application described Ardia primarily as a claims denial management tool for clinical labs. Since that submission (December 31, 2025), we have conducted discovery conversations with lab directors across the DFW area and studied 2025 peer-reviewed literature on lab billing failures. What we learned fundamentally expanded our scope: the highest-value and most underserved problem in independent lab revenue is not general claim denial — it is the extreme billing complexity surrounding precision medicine testing: pharmacogenomics (PGx), next-generation sequencing (NGS), and molecular diagnostics, where denial rates reach 35% and independent labs face 2.76× higher odds of denial than hospitals (JAMA Network Open, April 2025, n=29,919 claims).

It is a deepening based on real evidence from our target market. The mission remains identical: make independent clinical laboratories financially whole by recovering revenue they earned but lost to billing system failures.

What We Do
