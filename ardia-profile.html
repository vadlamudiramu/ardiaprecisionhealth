🧪 Products

Four AI Modules. One Platform.

Each module is purpose-built for a specific lab specialty — not adapted from generic RCM software. Powered by the shared Neuro-Symbolic architecture and deployable independently or as a full suite.

ToxIQ™
Toxicology Revenue Intelligence

Purpose-built for independent toxicology labs. Automates denial prevention and appeal generation for urine drug screens (G0480–G0483), definitive drug testing (80300–80377), and specimen validity testing. Addresses the most common tox denial: CO-50 (medical necessity not established) by cross-referencing LCD L36022, patient Rx records, and clinical documentation. Special focus on synthetic opioid detection documentation failures — responsible for 80%+ of tox billing errors.

Urine Drug Screens G0480–G0483 LCD L36022 CO-50 Appeals MAT/OTP Billing
MolecuIQ™
Molecular Diagnostics RCM

The most technically differentiated module. Automates MolDX/DEX Z-code registration management, CPT mapping across 75,000+ genetic tests to under 200 molecular pathology codes, LCD compliance verification, and Technical Assessment tracking. Handles PLA codes (0001U–0400U+), NGS prior authorization under CMS-0057-F, and NCCN/ASCO guideline citation for medical necessity arguments. Covers 7 MAC jurisdictions and commercial payer DEX adoption (UHC, Humana, MVP).

MolDX/DEX Z-codes NGS/PGx CPT 81400–81479 LCD L38337 CMS-0057-F PLA Codes
AcoIQ™
ACO Shared Savings Optimization

Designed for ACOs under MSSP Enhanced Track and ACO REACH models. Identifies missed HCC risk adjustment codes, flags patients at risk for high-cost episodes, and generates documentation for shared savings attribution. Targets DFW's best-in-class ACOs — Baylor Scott & White Quality Alliance ($107.6M MSSP savings) and Southwestern Health Resources (#1 nationally for savings four consecutive years, $168M+ cumulative).

HCC Coding MSSP Enhanced ACO REACH Risk Adjustment DFW ACOs
BehaviorIQ™
Behavioral Health & Addiction Medicine Billing

Addresses the unique billing complexity of behavioral health organizations, addiction medicine clinics (OTPs), and MAT providers. Handles H-codes (Medicaid behavioral health), J-codes (medication-assisted treatment — methadone, buprenorphine), E/M documentation for psychiatric visits, and CCBHC billing requirements. Target: DFW's 40+ behavioral health organizations including Metrocare Services (50,000+ patients/year) and Behavioral Health Group (117 locations nationally).

H-codes/J-codes MAT/OTP Billing CCBHC Medicaid BH Buprenorphine
