## The DGP Architecture — Core Contribution

**Inventor:** Rambabu Vadlamudi | **Domain:** Healthcare Revenue Cycle Management (RCM)

> Note: "Neuro-symbolic" is an extensively published academic term with prior art in diagnosis and clinical decision support. The DGP architecture is Ram's original coinage, differentiated by domain (administrative RCM, not clinical diagnosis) and specific implementation.

**Layer 1 — Deterministic Policy Engine**
- 847 rules covering all LCD/NCD/MolDX policies
- DEX Z-codes, CPT 81400–81479 series, PLA codes
- Zero hallucinations on policy interpretation

**Layer 2 — Generative Clinical Reasoning**
- LLM (Anthropic Claude API)
- Appeal briefs generated in <90 seconds
- Retrieves evidence from 14 medical databases in 340ms

**Layer 3 — Predictive Denial Prevention**
- ML model trained on historical EDI 835 claims data
- Pre-submission denial risk scoring

**What makes it original:** All prior hybrid AI healthcare architecture papers address clinical DIAGNOSIS. DGP applies the three-layer design to healthcare ADMINISTRATIVE automation (claim denial recovery) under MolDX/LCD/NCD/DEX Z-code compliance — a domain with no prior published work of this kind.
